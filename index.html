I'm Yogesh, a Doctoral Researcher at QQML group at Aalto University with Prof. Vikas Garg, Prof. Samuel Kaski, and Fr. Markus Heinonen focusing on physics-inspired deep learning for modeling dynamical systems, (geometric and topological) deep learning, generative models and robust bayesian inference.

I am also a recipient of Nokia Scholarship and a DAAD AI-Net Fellow.
